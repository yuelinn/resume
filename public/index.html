Selected Projects

Unsupervised Generation of Image-Label pairs

  • Most autonomous agricultural robots rely on machine vision systems. Such systems, however, often perform worse in new fields or when the robotic platforms change. While we can alleviate the performance degradation by manually labeling more data obtained in the new setup, this procedure is labor and cost-intensive. Therefore, we propose an approach to improve the performance of machine vision systems for new fields and different robotic platforms without additional manual labeling.
  • Check out our paper at ICRA'24!
  • Keywords: GAN, Domain shift

Bee Detection

  • We detect bees to investigate the biodiversity services of different cultivars. Detecting the bees is so challenging that even manual labels are prone to human errors. I am working on using deep learning models trained with erroneous labels to improve the label quality iteratively. The high-quality labels are then used to answer scientific questions regarding the cultivars' impact on biodiversity services. This is on-going work.
  • Keywords: AI-assisted labelling, Biodiversity

Data sets for Agriculture Robotics

  • We need diverse agriculture-related data sets to train and test our machine learning methods. I am coordinating efforts under PhenoRob to develop a diverse dataset which contains multi-platform, multi-sensor, multi-crops across mutliple growth stages. This is on-going work.
  • Interested in data to train ML for AgBots? Check out our dataset PhenoBench, which I co-authored.
  • Keywords: Data for ML, Agriculture

Diffusion Model for Anomaly Detection

  • Anomaly detection enables the detection of unexpected plants or obstacles in Agriculture and Phenotyping fields. I am working on using a Diffusion Model for anomaly detection in the fields. This is on-going work.
  • Keywords: DDPM, Anomaly detection
